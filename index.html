Our Mission and Vision

Mission

The first school of the Cathedral Parish of Imus, Cavite, Our Lady of the Pillar Catholic School, Inc. is commited to the Christian values formation and holistic development of children, capable of transforming the changing world.

Vision

Our Lady of the Pillar Catholic School, Inc. in-visions of Christian community, that is MAKA-DIYOS, MAKA-TAO, MAKA-BAYAN, MAKA-BUHAY, at MAKA-KALIKASAN. United in God becoming the church of the poor.

Our Graduates shall be:

  • Christ-centered and family oriented
  • Accountable and stewards of God's creation
  • Warm and simple
  • Clean and healthy in lifestyle
  • Innovative and critical thinkers
  • Eloquent communicators
  • Responsible in modern technology
  • Innovative and resourceful
  • Purposeful and decisive
  • Ethical and moral servant leaders
  • Globally competitive and locally active
  • Loyal and grateful to their Alma Mater
